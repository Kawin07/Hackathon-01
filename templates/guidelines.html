Hackathon Guidelines

Eligibility:

Participation is open to students currently enrolled in PEC EEE.

Team Formation:

Each team can have 1-4 members. Ensure that all team members' details are accurately entered in the registration form.

Registration Deadline:

Submit your registration form by Feb 20, 2025. Late entries will not be accepted.

Registration Process:

Fill in the registration form and choose your preferred domain.

Shortlisting Process:

Submitted ideas will be reviewed by the TechDivathon panel. Shortlisted participants will receive a confirmation email with further details regarding the TechDivathon.

Hackathon Details:

The 24-hour TechDivathon will be conducted on-site for shortlisted participants. Bring all required tools and equipment, including laptops, chargers, and any additional items you may need.

Project Development Rules:

You will have 24 hours to work on your project during the TechDivathon. Originality is key:

  • Projects must be your own creation and not plagiarized.
  • You may use pre-existing libraries or frameworks, but the final implementation must be unique.

Domain Selection:

Choose a domain from the given options during registration. Ensure your project aligns with the chosen domain and the problem statement.

Communication:

Keep your email active for updates regarding shortlisting and further instructions. All communication regarding the event will be shared via the registered email addresses.

General Rules:

  • Originality is mandatory. Plagiarized ideas will lead to disqualification.
  • Ensure that the domain and problem statement selected during registration are followed throughout the competition.

By following these guidelines, you can ensure a smooth registration and a fair chance of being shortlisted for the on-site hackathon (Sparkthon).
