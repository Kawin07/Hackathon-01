About Sparkathon

Sparkathon is a national-level 48-hour hackathon organized by the EEE Department of Panimalar Engineering College. It serves as a platform for innovators, developers, and problem-solvers to collaborate, create, and compete in building real-world solutions using cutting-edge technology.

Participants will have the opportunity to work with industry experts and mentors, enhance their technical skills, and win exciting rewards. The event fosters creativity, teamwork, and technical excellence, making it an unmissable experience for tech enthusiasts.
